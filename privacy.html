SheetSnap Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 6, 2026 · Last updated: June 6, 2026
This is the app-specific privacy policy for SheetSnap. It supplements the general DigiRune Studios Privacy Policy. Where the two address SheetSnap differently, this page — the more specific one — governs.

SheetSnap is a mobile app by DigiRune Studios, a division of McCullough Development, Inc. (a South Carolina corporation based in Beaufort, South Carolina, USA). The app lets you photograph a paper tabletop role-playing game (TTRPG) character sheet and turn it into an editable digital character that syncs across your devices. This policy explains, in plain language, what information the app handles and how. We built SheetSnap to respect your privacy: no ads, no tracking, and we never sell your data.

The short version. You sign in with your Apple or Google account so your characters can sync across your devices. We store your characters securely in the cloud, tied to your account, and a copy stays on your device so the app works offline. When you scan a sheet, the photos are sent once to our AI provider only to read the text on them — they are not used to train any AI and are deleted shortly after. We don't show ads, we don't sell your information, and you can delete your account and all your data from inside the app at any time.

1. Information we handle

Your account

To use SheetSnap you sign in with Sign in with Apple or Google. We receive a unique account identifier from that provider (and, depending on the provider and your choices, a name and/or email address) so we can recognize you and link your characters to your account. We use a trusted authentication service, Google Firebase Authentication, to manage sign-in securely. We do not receive or store your Apple or Google password.

Your character data

The digital characters you create or edit (names, stats, notes, optional portrait images, themes, and session journal entries) are stored in two places: securely in the cloud using Google Firebase / Firestore, tied to your account so they sync across your devices, and as a local copy on each device so the app works offline. Your character data is private to your account; we do not browse, share, or sell it.

Character photos you scan

When you take or upload photos of a character sheet and tap Scan, those images are transmitted over a secure connection to our processing service (see Section 2) so the text on the page can be extracted into digital fields. We do not permanently store your photos on our servers, and the app does not keep the original images after extraction — only the structured character data it produces.

Purchases

SheetSnap is a paid app and offers optional in-app purchases (additional character slots). All payments and purchase records are handled directly by the Apple App Store or Google Play under their own terms and privacy policies. DigiRune Studios never sees or stores your payment card details.

What we do not collect

  • No advertising identifiers, and no third-party advertising or analytics tracking SDKs.
  • No location data, contacts, or microphone access.
  • Camera and photo access are used only when you choose to add a page.
  • We do not build a profile of you or track you across other apps or websites.

2. Our AI processing service

To read the text on your character sheets, SheetSnap sends the photos to Anthropic, the maker of the Claude AI models, through a secure server we operate. We use Anthropic's commercial API for this single purpose: turning the writing on your page into editable fields.

Per Anthropic's commercial API terms, inputs and outputs sent through the API are not used to train Anthropic's AI models, and they are automatically deleted from Anthropic's systems after a short retention period (currently about 7 days) kept only to detect misuse. Your character photos are never used to build or improve any AI model. Anthropic's data practices are described at privacy.claude.com.

3. How your information is used

  • To sign you in and recognize your account across your devices.
  • To extract the text from your character sheet photos and build your digital character.
  • To store and sync your characters so they appear on all your devices.
  • To process optional in-app purchases through the app stores.

We do not use your information for advertising, profiling, or any purpose beyond operating the app's core features.

4. Service providers we rely on

We do not sell, rent, or trade your information. We share data only with the service providers needed to run the app, each for the limited purpose described:

  • Google Firebase (Authentication & Firestore) — to sign you in and store/sync your characters securely.
  • Anthropic — to read your scan photos and return the extracted text, as described in Section 2.
  • Cloudflare — the secure server that relays scan requests to Anthropic.
  • Apple / Google — sign-in and purchase processing.

We may also disclose information if required by law.

5. Data security & retention

Sign-in and cloud storage are handled through Google Firebase's secure infrastructure, and scan photos are transmitted over encrypted (HTTPS) connections. We keep your account and character data for as long as your account is active. When you delete your account (see Section 6), your characters and account record are removed from our cloud storage.

6. Your choices & rights

  • Delete a character at any time from within the app; the change syncs to your other devices.
  • Delete your account and all your data from inside the app — this permanently removes your characters and account record from our cloud storage.
  • Export a backup of any character as a file before deleting.
  • Sign out on any device to remove the local copy from that device.
  • Decline camera or photo access in your device settings at any time.

To request help with your data, contact us at the address in Section 9.

7. Children's privacy

SheetSnap is intended for a general audience and is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us information, contact us and we will delete it.

8. Changes to this policy

If we materially change how we handle your information, we will update this policy and revise the date above, and where appropriate notify you in the app, before the change takes effect.
